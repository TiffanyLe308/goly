Data Collected Automatically

Certain technical information may be collected automatically as a standard part of your use of the Goly Platform and this information may in certain circumstances constitute personal data.

Automatically collected data may include:

  • Our servers automatically record information (“Log Data“). The logging technology used in the Goly Platform automatically collects the URL of the site from which you came and the site to which you are going when you leave the Goly Platform. We also collect the Internet protocol (IP) address of the device you use to access the Goly Platform, information about the operating system of your device, as well as the name of your Internet service provider, browser type, the referring web page, pages visited, location, your mobile carrier, device and application IDs, search terms, and cookie information. We receive Log Data when you interact with our Goly Platform, for example, when you visit our website, sign into our Goly Platform, or interact with our email notifications. Goly uses Log Data to provide our Goly Platform and to measure, customize, and improve them. We will either delete Log Data or remove any common account identifiers, such as your username, full IP address, or email address, within or after 18 months.
  • In addition to server collected log data, Goly uses analytics software to track and report user behavior anonymously through user log information. This data provides us additional information needed to evaluate how our service is used in order to improve our services. This report does not include personally identifiable data. The software has access to this data to process the information earlier stated on behalf of Goly.
  • Cookies are text files that are saved on the hard drive of your device by means of your browser, enabling us to recognize your browser. Goly may use both session cookies and persistent cookies to better understand how you interact with our Goly Platform, to monitor aggregate usage by our users and web traffic routing on our Goly Platform, and to customize and improve our Goly Platform. We may place a cookie on the hard drive of the device that you use to access the Goly Platform. Most of the currently available browsers give you the option of managing cookies by, for example, disabling them entirely, accepting them individually, and deleting saved cookies from your hard drive. We do not require cookies for many parts of our Goly Platform such as viewing public videos, but we would like to remind you that if you completely disable cookies on your browser, you might not be able to use some features of the Goly Platform.
  • We may keep track of how you interact with links across our Goly Platform, including our email notifications, third-party services, and client applications, by redirecting clicks or through other means. We do this to help improve our Goly Platform, for example to provide relevant advertising, and to be able to share aggregate click statistics such as how many times a particular link was clicked on.

Moreover, details of transactions you carry out through the Goly Platform, and details of your visits to the Goly Platform including, but not limited to, traffic data and other communication data may also be collected.

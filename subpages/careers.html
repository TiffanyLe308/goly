Working at Goly

Goly is a place for people from all walks of life. At Goly, we strive to become the world’s platform for people to find their voice by building products and services that empower them to do so. Advancement in technology has changed the way we live today, all across the globe. Goly has identified crucial areas, that if powered by technology, authentic services can help businesses and consumers achieve their goals.

In our journey, we

Do something different

Though our projects might be aligned with trends, we ensure what we do is making the lives of others better.

Relax and snack

When we can’t make them, we buy them. We bake cakes, make paninis and ensure you can snack without losing focus on work. Chats don’t distract us, they connect us.

Collaborate

Rather than look inward only, we seek outside perspectives to solve challenges together with our customers and partners.

Experiment and learn

We love to learn and without trying, we wouldn’t. So we design experiments, check our dashboards and act on facts.

Partner

We employ and partner once we can validate a commitment to our vision and goals. So join us and become a partner.

Are customer-centric

Our service providers and seekers are at the centre of everything we do. If they can’t benefit from what we do, we know we won’t be here for too long.

Get things done

We have an agnostic approach to planning. We plan and do at the same time. In a fast changing environment, we know agility can keep us ahead.

Are solution-focused

Rather than let problems drown us, we seek innovative solutions to them. After all, this is what our customers hired us to do.

Are innovative

We approach challenges with open mindset and frugal thinking. In Goly, all ideas are welcomed and treated equally since there is no such thing as bad idea.

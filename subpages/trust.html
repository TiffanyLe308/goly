Trust and safety

Updated on 25th April, 2016

The entire Goly community is based on trust. Therefore, we have ensured every profile on Goly is verified using their mobile phone number they have provided us. In addition, we have taken additional steps to verify Sellers by email, their Facebook ID (optional), personal identity and personal interview. Goly Sellers are rated and reviewed after every successful transaction to ensure quality service delivery. However, we recommend the following guidelines and recommendations to ensure your safety every time you interact with Sellers and Buyers via Goly marketplace.

Services on Goly

We currently support event related services of all sizes including skill based services, equipment and venue rentals. All services outside the following categories are not allowed on Goly.

Goly is for services

Goly is a platform that connects Service-based Sellers with customers. Therefore, we do not support product-based businesses such as sales of mobile phones, computers, clothes and so on.

Where do I get served?

Services on Goly are delivered face-to-face and not completely online. Goly is only a platform that connects Buyers and Sellers and the delivery happens at an agreed location between both parties. Goly is a location-based platform. This means whenever you run a search, Sellers are delivered to you relative to the location of the service and your location, usually within your city. We aim to reduce the time it takes you to get whatever is it that you need.

List only your services

On Goly, list only the services that you provide yourself. We do not allow referrals listings on behalf of other services such as directory listings and multi-level marketing systems (MLM)

Do not list anything sexual

Goly does not allow listing of sexual services such as prostitution, sexual massage and any unethical services

No minors

You have to be at least 18 years old to be eligible to offer services through Goly.

Services on Goly are local and within cities

Currently, Goly only allow local services between people in a country. DO NOT ACCEPT CONTACTS FROM OUTSIDE YOUR COUNTRY. When you exchange payment for service, ensure that the Seller is from the Country and not from another country.

Only English profiles

The official language on Goly is English. Goly accepts situations where a Sellers mixes English and local languages for better expression. Goly does not accept responsibility for any misunderstanding between a Seller and Buyer.

How do I decide whom to use for my service?

We perform verification with mobile phone for every user on Goly. However, we recommend the following guidelines when you deal with Sellers and Buyers.

Review from past customers on Goly

We do our best to ensure every Seller on Goly is reviewed after every successful connection. Use this reviews to your advantage by reading them before contacting a Sellers

Number of matches on Goly

When Goly delivers appropriate Sellers after a search, you can see the number of ratings and reviews. Use this to evaluate the skill level of the Sellers.

Photos of past work

Goly allows any Sellers to upload work samples in the form of images. These pictures speak a lot about the previous work of the Sellers. Use them to evaluate the quality of work the service Seller can provide.

Identity card when you request services

At your discretion, you can ask for identification when you are in doubt. Forms of identification include valid international passport, national identity card and driver’s license. Look at the face and picture and ensure they match

Connection to social media accounts

A quick verification on social media such as Facebook can help you see people’s identity.

Verified contact information

We ensure that every mobile phone number provided on Goly is verified. This adds an extra layer of security to the Goly community

Evaluate the professional's expertise

Some Sellers on Goly will have professional licenses to prove their expertise. Where applicable and when it helps you to make better decision on whom to hire, we recommend you request for such licenses at the point of service delivery.

Use your good judgement

Goly works very hard to ensure that the information provided by sellers on Goly are genuine. However, we do not guarantee that our limited review process is enough. So use your good judgement and exercise caution when hiring professionals. Be aware of professionals who severely undercut the competition on pricing, or who do not have required credentials to provide a great service.

Goly does not insure against losses from work delivered by services on Goly.
